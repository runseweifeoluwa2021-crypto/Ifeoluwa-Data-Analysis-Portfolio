# Adidas Sales Dataset Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes Adidas sales data to uncover performance trends, product insights, regional strengths, and sales channel opportunities. The dataset contains a clean **Sales Fact Table** with:

- Retailer  
- Retailer ID  
- Invoice Date  
- Region  
- State  
- City  
- Product  
- Price per Unit  
- Units Sold  
- Total Sales  
- Operating Profit  
- Operating Margin  
- Sales Method (In-store, Outlet, Online)  

## Key Business Insights

### Overall Performance
- **Total Revenue:** $899.9M  
- **Total Units Sold:** 2.48M  
- **Total Operating Profit:** $332.1M  
- **Average Operating Margin:** 42.3%  

> Adidas shows high profitability, strong margin performance.

### Top Performing Products (by Revenue)
1. Men’s Street Footwear – $208.8M  
2. Women’s Apparel – $179.0M  
3. Men’s Athletic Footwear – $153.7M  
4. Women’s Street Footwear – $128.0M  
5. Men’s Apparel – $123.7M  

> Footwear drives revenue, with street footwear outperforming athletic.

### Regional Performance
- **West:** $269.9M (dominant)  
- Northeast: $186.3M  
- Southeast: $163.1M  
- South: $144.6M  
- Midwest: $135.8M  

> West is the strongest market; targeted campaigns in other regions can unlock growth.

### Sales Channel Performance
- **In-store:** $356.6M  
- Outlet: $295.6M  
- Online: $247.7M  

> Physical retail dominates; online presents growth opportunities.

## Executive Recommendations
1. **Invest in Footwear, especially Street lines** – strongest revenue driver.  
2. **Target West region retention** and increase marketing efforts in Northeast, Southeast, South, and Midwest.  
3. **Enhance online sales strategy** – potential for digital growth.  
4. **Leverage high-margin products** for promotional bundles to maximize profitability.  
5. **Monitor In-store vs. Outlet performance** to optimize inventory allocation.

## Next Steps

- Explore product-level trends by seasonality and promotions.
